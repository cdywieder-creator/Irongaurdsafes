Services

Whatever you need to protect, we install the right safe — securely, discreetly, and to professional standards.

01Firearm

Gun Safes

Responsible firearm ownership starts with secure storage. We install heavy-duty gun safes that keep firearms away from children and intruders while staying instantly accessible to you. Every safe is properly anchored, leveled and positioned for both security and convenience.

  • Long-gun & handgun configurations
  • Bolt-down anchoring to floor or wall
  • Combination, electronic & biometric locks
  • Discreet placement guidance
02Valuables

Home Safes

From cash and jewelry to passports, deeds and family heirlooms, a quality home safe keeps your valuables protected and organized. We help you choose the right size and security rating, then mount and anchor it so it can't simply be carried off.

  • Right-sized for your valuables
  • Anchored & tamper-resistant
  • Closet, cabinet or concealed placement
  • Easy day-to-day access
03Fire Rated

Fireproof Safes

A house fire can destroy irreplaceable documents and data in minutes. Fire-rated safes are engineered to maintain safe internal temperatures during a blaze, protecting paper records, hard drives, photos and keepsakes. We install them where they'll perform best and stay easy to reach.

  • Fire- & heat-rated models
  • Document, media & data protection
  • Water-resistant options
  • Professional placement & anchoring
04Concealed

Wall & Floor Safes

For maximum discretion, nothing beats a safe built into your home. We install in-wall and in-floor safes that sit flush and out of sight — behind artwork, inside closets, or beneath flooring — giving you a hidden layer of security intruders won't even know exists.

  • Concealed in-wall installation
  • In-floor & under-flooring options
  • Flush, seamless finishing
  • Structural assessment included
05Quick Access

Biometric Safes

Modern biometric safes open with the touch of a fingerprint — fast for you, impossible for everyone else. We install and program biometric models so they're reliable, secure and ready when seconds count. Ideal for bedside firearm storage and quick-access valuables.

  • Fingerprint & multi-user enrollment
  • Backup key & keypad access
  • Fast, reliable entry
  • Setup & user training included
