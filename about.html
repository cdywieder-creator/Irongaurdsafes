Meet The Founder

Hi, I'm Nuchem Braun.

I started Ironguard Safes for one reason: I believe every family deserves to feel safe in their own home. When a client calls me, they're not just buying a safe — they're trusting me to help protect their loved ones, their hard-earned valuables, and the things that can never be replaced. I don't take that lightly.

For me, safety isn't a sales pitch — it's the first priority on every single job. Before I think about anything else, I think about what would keep my own family secure, and I hold every installation to that standard. I'd rather take the extra time to do it right than cut a corner that someone counts on later.

I also believe that how you do the work matters as much as the work itself. When I'm in someone's home, I treat it with the same care and respect I'd want in mine — clean, considerate, and honest about what you do and don't need. No pressure, no upselling, just straight answers and solid craftsmanship.

Ironguard has grown the way I think a security business should: one satisfied client at a time. Every referral and every repeat call means the trust people placed in me was well-founded — and that's the reputation I'm proud to keep building, one installation at a time.

— Nuchem Braun
